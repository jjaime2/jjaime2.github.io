Showcasing 2 years of my personal projects and experience as an embedded systems engineer.

UW-X

I am currently an embedded systems intern at UW-X where I am working to develop cool projects largely on the Nordic Semiconductor platform. I have developed firmware and apps for use in BLE RSSI tracking and even streaming microphone data through BLE.

Learn more

UW Biorobotics

Over the past year I have been a part of UWs Biorobotics as an undergraduate research student. Here, I've devoted countless hours to developing the hardware and firmware for a custom pulse oximeter. This pulse oximeter system will be mounted on a grasper which can aid in surgery by detecting local ischemia in the patient.

Learn more

Team Lifeline

My embedded systems capstone built off my previous experience with pulse oximetry. Here, I worked alongside 3 other embedded systems students to create an ambitious wearable device. The goal of this project was to create a prototype device that would alert patients in advance of a potential hypoxic event. We thought this was particularly relevant during this ongoing epidemic, and I have personally chosen to continue development of this project beyond just the scope of this class.
